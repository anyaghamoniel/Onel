About AntCastle Agro Commodities

Building trusted connections between Nigerian agricultural suppliers and global buyers.

Who We Are

AntCastle Agro Commodities is a Nigerian agricultural sourcing company focused on connecting international buyers with trusted Nigerian suppliers of export-quality agricultural commodities.


Our mission is to simplify sourcing by building long-term relationships, maintaining professional communication, and helping businesses discover reliable sourcing opportunities across Nigeria.
