About Us

Excellence in Agro Commodity Export

Global Harvest Export Ltd is a leading Nigerian agro-commodity export company dedicated to sourcing, processing, and exporting premium agricultural products from West Africa to global markets.

We maintain full traceability from farm to port, ensuring compliance with international quality standards including HACCP, ISO 22000, and FDA requirements. Our commitment to sustainable sourcing and ethical trade practices makes us the preferred partner for importers worldwide.
